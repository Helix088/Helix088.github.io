Become a Member Today!

Becoming a member of the Chamber helps improve the Pride, LA community and local businesses grow. As a one time offer joining will give you a 10% discount* on your membership for the first year.

*Must be at least be member for one full year in order to qualify. Discount only applies to new members.

Purpose:

The purpose of this Chamber is to help build Pride into the city that we know it can become. To do this we will work with small and large businesses. We want it to be a thriving city that will help boost the economy of itself and the state.

Goals:

  1. Establish 1 business in Pride every quarter.
  2. Hold at least one charitable event every 2 months.
  3. Increase employement rates in Pride by 5% every year.
