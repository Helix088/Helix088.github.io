The Audience

The target audience are people in Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven and want to know the weather, but do not want to use the website instead of their phone. This website specifically targets the communities in theses cities.

Scenario 1

My family and I are going to go white-water rafting in a week. We want to see where the best place to go rafting in southern Idaho. We want to know what the weather will be like around that time. We come to www.seidahoinfo.com to check and see if we will be able to go. We do not want to go if it is raining or cloudy. We are hoping the temperature is about 80 degrees Farenheit. If it is below this the water will be too cold and we will not be able to enjoy ourselves.

Scenario 2

I am looking for the best place to shop where I live. I want to look for dresses and shoes. Is there a mall or outlet stores in the area? If not which stores are best for what I am looking for? I visit the website to see if I can find the best place to shop. Also, so I can see which stores have the cheapest prices.

Scenario 3

My son plays for the football team at Preston High School. I was not able to attend the game, and I want to know the details. I go to the website and find the section for my sons school to find out the score, the best plays, and etc.

Scenario 4

I am a college student who does not know how to cook and I recently moved to Soda from Big Piney, Wyoming. I got accepted to the Idaho State University-Soda Springs, so I could stay close enough to my family. I do not know this area too well and I saw this site and was wondering what the best place to go for dinner was.

Scenario 5

I am in need of a passport, but I can not find the place I need to go. I was told that I could use this website to find a place that provides passport services. I see there is a section for government buildings on this website. I look under my towns page, Fish Haven, to find the nearest governemt building.
